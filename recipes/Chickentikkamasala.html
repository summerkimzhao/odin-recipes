Easy Instant Pot Chicken Tikka Masala

This is definitely one of the easiest Chicken Tikka Masala recipe out there. It's not really authentic, but it's quick and easy, so I definitely recommend making it when you are busy!

Servings:10-12

Ingredients

  • 5 jars of Target Tikka masala sauce
  • 2 15oz cans of chick peas
  • 1 onion
  • 3-5lb of Chicken (any type is fine. I prefer using chicken thights

Steps

  1. Saute onions in olive oil
  2. Pour all the sauce, chick peas, and chicken
  3. Add 2 cups of water/coonut milk/coconut water
  4. Cook for 8min high pressure

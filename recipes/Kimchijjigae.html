Kimchi Jjigae (Korean Kimchi Soup)

There are so many ways to make Kimchijjigae. I have several recipes, but this is a good one to feed a crowd as Spam is a relatively cheaper protein option. I never took a photo of my Kimchi jjigae before. I'll update the photo soon.

Servings:10-12

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of Spam
  • 5 cups of Kimchi (preferably well fermented one)
  • 1 onion
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 cup of mushrooms of your choice (I prefer dried shirataki)
  • 2 soft tofu
  • 1 dashi pack
  • 2 Tbs soy sauce
  • 2 Tbs fish sauce

Steps

  1. In a large dutch oven, stir fry cubed spam
  2. Once the spam is brown and they let out some oil, add chopped kimchi and onion
  3. Once onion looks transculent and kimchi looks cooked, add water, dashi pack, 2 Tbs of soy sauce and 2 Tbs of Korean fish sauce
  4. Once the soup boils, reduce the heat to medium and let it boil for another 15-20mins
  5. Add chopped zuccini and musrooms and let it boil for another 10min
  6. Add cubed tofu and let it boil for another 10min
  7. Serve with rice!
